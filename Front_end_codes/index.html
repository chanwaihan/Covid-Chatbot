A Question-Answering System about COVID

Presented By: Group MCS15

Introduction

In late 2019, a virus named SARS-CoV-2 emerged from Wuhan, China, which resulted in a world pandemic. The disease was officially named Covid-19 or Coronavirus by the World Health Organization. Coronavirus is an infectious disease that transmits via droplet or contact transmission, and many people might not have the appropriate knowledge, acceptance, and perception of Covid.

Project Objective

The objective of our project is to create a question and answering system that can provide information related to covid and a repository for self browsing.
